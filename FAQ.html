Any damaged or wrong orders should be returned within 2 weeks for exchange or full refund. For other goods returned withon 30 days, refunds up to 90% of the original value and an exchange for good of equal or less value are allowed.

We ship our products to all major cities in East Africa. Our CCTV/VSAT installation services, however, are only available in Kenya.
