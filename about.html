How it all started

Founded in 2019 by Hannan Ibrahim, FocalPoint Distinction has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office, a small room in a 2 bedroom apartment in Nairobi,Kenya. When Hannan first started out, her passion for helping small and medium size business ventures optimize and upgrade their day-to-day running by providing customized digital solutions and affordable, quality equipment drove her to do intense research about the needs of local businesses and to eventually quit her day job, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Kenya and East Africa, and are thrilled to be a part of the rapidly-expanding small business economy.Since its humble beginnings, FocalPoint Distinction has grown from a single office and 3-person work force to a team of over 50 competent designers, technicians and skilled men and women, with offices in 5 major cities and as many warehouses.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
